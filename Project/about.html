Who We Are

We are Momentum Gym, a state-of-the-art fitness facility committed to helping you achieve your health and fitness goals. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced athlete, we offer personalized training, group classes, and all the tools you need for success.

Meet Our Team

Sarah Smith
Strength Training & HIIT

Sarah is a certified personal trainer with over 7 years of experience in helping clients achieve their fitness goals through strength training and high-intensity interval training (HIIT). With a passion for empowering individuals to push their limits, Sarah uses a combination of tailored workout plans and motivational coaching to maximize results. She believes in building strength both physically and mentally, and loves seeing her clients transform into the best versions of themselves. Sarah is dedicated to providing a supportive and challenging environment, where each client can succeed at their own pace.
